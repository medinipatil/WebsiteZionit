RFID: The Technology Making Industries Smarter

Radio Frequency Identification or RFID is a specific type of radio technology that uses radio waves to identify tags attached to an object and thus identify the object.
The key to an RFID strategy is to align your use of the technology with your company's goals, e.g., reduce production costs, better customer relationships, optimising operations, and more.
